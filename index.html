Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the Black Edition different?

The Black Edition features exclusive darkened exterior accents, premium black leather interior with Alcantara accents, enhanced performance tuning, and sport-tuned suspension for a truly unique and aggressive driving experience.

What's the fuel consumption?

WLTP combined consumption is 10.8 L/100km with emissions of 272 g CO2/km. Real-world consumption varies based on driving style, road conditions, and traffic. The hybrid system helps optimize fuel efficiency.

Is warranty included?

Yes, all Black Edition vehicles come with 3 years/unlimited miles basic warranty, extendable up to 5 years with optional premium packages. Includes all components including hybrid system and battery.

Can I customize the vehicle?

Absolutely! Porsche offers extensive customization through our Porsche Exclusive Manufaktur program, including colors, materials, wheels, interior finishes, and performance packages.

What are the engine specifications?

The Black Edition features a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine with 48V hybrid system, producing 455 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. Combined with the electric motor, it delivers enhanced performance and efficiency.

How fast does it accelerate?

The Cayenne Black Edition accelerates from 0-60 mph in 5.2 seconds with a top speed of 157 mph. Advanced all-wheel drive system ensures superior traction and handling in all conditions.

What financing options are available?

We offer flexible Porsche Finance programs with terms from 24-84 months, competitive lease options, trade-in programs, and special promotional rates. Exclusive banking partnerships provide premium financing solutions.

Is the vehicle environmentally friendly?

Yes. The 48V hybrid system reduces emissions and fuel consumption while maintaining exceptional performance. The vehicle meets strict Euro 6d emissions standards with advanced particulate and NOx reduction technologies.
